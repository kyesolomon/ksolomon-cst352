BS in Computer Science @ Cal State Monterey Bay

Software Engineering Network & Security Game Development

A Computer Science major teaches students with a familiarity of
basic computer science knowledge paired with applicable information
on IT skills. Students are trained for jobs in computing technology
industries or to get accepted into grad school.

Here, students learn the most basic CS concepts
while applying what they've learned to solve problems. Collaborating,
team work and speaking effectively are included in the learning curriculum
with mostly projects in a state-of-the-art BIT building w/ lots of PC's.
Students start by getting similar sets of core classes to establish a strong
foundation in CS, then by taking classes in one of three
specific areas: software engineering, network and security, or game development.
The requirements give students the chance to
get concepts and learn skills in not just a single concentration, making
them to well rounded CS pro's if they graduate.
